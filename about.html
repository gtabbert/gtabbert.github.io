About

I am a designer based in Eastern Washington. I have spent a good part of my life living in Spokane, and I have a passion for adventure and exploration.

Exploring the world around me is one of my favorite hobbies. My design and photography style is meant to encourage others to be more observant about their environment and to go out and explore every once in a while.
